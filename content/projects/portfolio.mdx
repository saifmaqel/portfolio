### Overview

A fast, modern, and content-driven **portfolio site** designed to showcase my projects, and experience in a clean, organized, and interactive way.  
Built with **Next.js**, **React**, **Tailwind CSS**, and **MDX** for flexible content management. The site is fully responsive, SEO-optimized, and capable of embedding live React components within project and experience pages.

### Achievements / Highlights

- Developed a **highly modular portfolio system** with dynamic project pages and MDX-powered.
- Implemented **search and filter functionality** to help visitors quickly find relevant projects or topics.
- Styled with **Tailwind CSS** for a responsive, utility-first design.
- Used **Next.js dynamic routing** to automatically generate project and experience pages from MDX files.
- Deployed on **Vercel** with CI/CD, automated previews, and global edge caching for ultra-fast loading.
- Ensured **cross-device compatibility**, with smooth layouts for mobile, tablet, and desktop.
